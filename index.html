LA Wildfires Force Thousands To Evacuate

Raging fires across Los Angeles county have forced 153,000 residents to evacuate their homes, according to LA County.

The wildfires are being fueled by unprecedented high winds of up to 50 mph, according to the National Weather Service (NWS). This has caused the NWS to place much of Southern California under a red flag warning due to hazardous conditions.

NBC4 Los Angeles has tracked the total acreage of the four major active fires in LA county, including:

  • Hurst Fire: 799 acres
  • Kenneth Fire; 1,052 acres
  • Eaton Fire: 14,117 acres
  • Palisades Fire: 22,660 acres
